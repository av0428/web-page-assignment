Social Media Platforms

Social media platforms have taken over the world, they include online tools and resources to provide personal accounts that allow people to share and express their lives on the internet. Some social media platforms include Instagram, Tiktok, Facebook, and Twitter. Social media offers many features that are accustomed to each indivicual. These features include photo sharing, video sharing, stautus updates, follower count, like count, messaging, live streaming, and content editing. Social media platforms have had some very positive impacts on businesses. Owners of businesses can use social media to market, sell, advertize, and provide customer infromation and support. Social media platforms have helped many people get themselves out there, including artists, celebrities, music producers, business owners, entrepreneurs, and content creators. A new phenomenon of jobs that have been on the rise recently due to social media are called "influencers". Influencers are individuals with social media platforms who are experts at a particular field, they use their platforms to endorse products, share content, and grow an audience. Although there are many benefits of social media platforms, there are also some societal concerns due to social media. Social media is very personal and public. This can cause many concerns regarding safety, privacy, and security. As well as these issues, social media is accountable for the spread of misinformation. Since everyone has social media, many people share their own facts and opinions, with nothing to back it up with. This causes a widespread of misinformation, resulting in confusion and hardships. In summary, social media has changed the way the world communicates and connects with one another- in good ways, and harmful ways.
